Making STEM Fun

EpiSTEM is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization that aims to cultivate a passion for the STEM (Science, Technology, Education, Math) fields among elementary school children. Our lab leaders go into elementary school classrooms and perform fun and interactive science labs with the students, aligning with the curriculum they are currently learning in class.

Our Impact

EpiSTEM constantly strives to maintain a positive impact and outreach within our communities that not only connects students, but teachers, administrators, and parents alike. We do this not only through our regular lab leading sessions with elementary school classes, but also through community events such as our STEM Night, and our summer school lab leading sessions.

General Lab Leading

McAuliffe, Van Horn, Whitley, and Laurelglen Elementary Schools
Our greatest impact lies in our general lab leading sessions throughout the school year. Our talented team of officers and lab leaders lead hands-on, interactive science labs with classes of students, currently across 4 elementary schools. These sessions spark curiosity and foster a love for science, making STEM more accessible and exciting for young minds. By bringing engaging experiments directly into classrooms, we aim to inspire the next generation of innovators and problem solvers.

Our STEM Night

Christa McAuliffe Elementary School - April 25th, 2024
At Christa McAuliffe Elementary School on April 25th, we hosted our STEM Night: a showcase of 14 different science labs that are akin to those usually done during lab leading sessions in classrooms. Students, parents, teachers, and school administrators were all able to see what EpiSTEM does and how we carry out our goals of cultivating a love for STEM among students.

Summer School Lab Leading

Christa McAuliffe Elementary School
We were given the wonderful opportunity of leading labs during 3rd and 4th grade summer school at Christa McAuliffe Elementary. Our lab leaders and officers gave the students an early, hands-on look at the science curriculum that they would be studying during the upcoming school year.

November STEAM Day

Christa McAuliffe Elementary School - November 1st, 2024
On the morning of, November 1, 2024 at McAuliffe Elementary School, our wonderful officers hosted a STEAM day for the students, complete with Halloween inspired science labs. The students explored exciting hands-on activities, from dancing raisins to mummy's magic milk. It was a memorable morning filled with a touch of Halloween magic!

Interested in becoming a Lab Leader?
